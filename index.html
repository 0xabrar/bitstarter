Preorder With Bitcoin

Optiol acts as a marketplace to bring together everyday buyers and sellers. Through Optiol, people looking for odd jobs to do, and those who have odd job that need to be done have a common place to search.

Finding local listings normally isn't so easy. You have to call countless people, search through endless lists, and overall waste a lot of time.

With Optiol, we're making that a lot easier .

Optiol is a work in progress. Although a full site isn't available for use at the moment, the team is constantly at work to bring this tool to the community.

FAQ

Why would I want to use this?
As we've stated earlier, bringing together buyers and sellers for odd jobs isn't so easy at the local level. The main use of Optiol is to provide a medium through which people can trade. But this isn't a regular trade of products we're talking about; we're going to be trading people's skills.
Why use Bitcoin?
Bitcoin is an up and coming global currency. Free of regulation and international boundary, Bitcoin is an excellent choice for creating a service which is meant to have a global impact.
